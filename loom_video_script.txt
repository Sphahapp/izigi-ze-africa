TIPS FOR RECORDING:

1. **Be Natural**: Don't read word-for-word - use this as a guide and speak naturally
2. **Show Enthusiasm**: Smile, use hand gestures, show your personality
3. **Eye Contact**: Look at the camera, not the screen
4. **Pace**: Speak clearly but don't rush - take natural pauses
5. **Environment**: Good lighting, quiet space, professional background
6. **Practice**: Record a test run first to get comfortable
7. **Length**: Aim for 2-3 minutes - concise but complete
8. **Energy**: Bring positive energy and show you're excited about the opportunity
