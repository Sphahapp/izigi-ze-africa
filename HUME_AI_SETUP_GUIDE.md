## üìù Script Markers Explained

All scripts use these markers that Hume AI will interpret naturally:

### Pauses
- `[PAUSE]` = Short pause (0.5-1 second)
- `[PAUSE - LONG]` = Longer pause (2-3 seconds)

**Usage Example:**
```
Welcome to IZIGI ze AFRICA [PAUSE] an AI Development Studio [PAUSE - LONG]
```

### Breathing
- `[BREATHE]` = Natural breathing moment

**Usage:**
```
[BREATHE]
This gives the voice time to breathe naturally...
```

### Gestures (Optional for Video)
- `[GESTURE: description]` = Physical gesture cue

**Note:** If you're creating video with gestures, these markers show when to gesture. If audio-only, Hume AI will naturally emphasize these moments.

### Emphasis
- `[EMPHASIS]` = Words to stress with voice

**Example:**
```
This is [EMPHASIS] truly revolutionary.
```

### Tone Changes
- `[TONE: description]` = Voice tone instruction

**Common tones used:**
- `[TONE: Welcoming, enthusiastic]`
- `[TONE: Serious, problem-focused]`
- `[TONE: Confident, solution-focused]`
- `[TONE: Technical, confident]`
- `[TONE: Business-focused, value-driven]`

## üé§ Hume AI Voice Settings Recommendations

### Recommended Voice Settings:

1. **Voice Type**: 
   - Choose a professional, clear voice
   - Male or female - your preference
   - Should sound authoritative but friendly

2. **Speed/Pace**:
   - **Moderate**: 150-180 words per minute
   - Not too fast (hard to follow)
   - Not too slow (loses engagement)

3. **Emphasis Handling**:
   - Hume AI will naturally emphasize `[EMPHASIS]` markers
   - No additional settings needed

4. **Pause Handling**:
   - Hume AI will respect `[PAUSE]` markers
   - Adjust timing if needed in post-processing

5. **Tone Variations**:
   - Hume AI will adapt tone based on `[TONE:]` markers
   - Test different voices to find best match

## üîß Post-Processing Tips

### Audio Editing After Hume AI:

1. **Normalize Volume**:
   - Ensure consistent volume throughout
   - No sudden volume jumps

2. **Remove Long Silence**:
   - Some `[PAUSE - LONG]` might be too long
   - Trim if needed for your pacing

3. **Add Subtle Background Music** (Optional):
   - Low volume (10-15% max)
   - Instrumental only
   - Should not distract from narration

4. **Quality Check**:
   - Listen through completely
   - Check pronunciation of technical terms
   - Verify emphasis is correct
